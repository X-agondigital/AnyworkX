How it Works

Download, Register and Get Verified

Sign up by downloading the app and provide all required information. Start your verification process to enable you enjoy all the benefits on the app. You must complete the basic verification levels to transact on the app.

Book a Service

Once customers are connected with their preferred service provider, both parties must negotiate terms via the app chat or call feature. All negotiations are done via the app for tracking, audit and dispute resolution.

Negotiate Fees With Service Providers

Connect with the service providers using the chat or call feature on the app to further discuss the work and close a deal.

Make Payment

Payment for transactions consummated via AnyworkX must be done through the in-app wallet. Customers are expected to fund their wallet with the full fee negotiated with the service provider. Once the service provider gets the job done, the customer’s wallet is debited and credited to the service provider’s wallet.

Rate Services

AnyworkX provides customers with the opportunity to rate the work of the service provider and how satisfied they are with the customer service provided. Ratings from customers are aggregated and become part of the profile of the service provider.

Why AnyWorkX?

Verified Personnel

AnyworkX prioritizes user safety and security by verifying the identities of service providers and customers to a reasonable extent. Different levels of verification are available, and users can choose the level of verification of the counterparty they are comfortable with before negotiation.

Secure Payment Method

AnyworkX guarantees a safe and secure payment method. The wallet feature of the app is to ensure seamless financial transaction between customers and service providers. Payment for service by the customer is escrowed in the customer's wallet until the job is done. This is to give comfort to both parties.

The Biggest Marketplace

AnyworkX is set to boost Africa's service economy by creating a huge marketplace with endless possibilities for users . Service providers have the opportunity to earn additional income and flexibility to transact business anytime 24/7. Customers benefit from the array of talents available on the App, and convenience and safety. AnyworkX is the right platform for service providers to list their business and showcase their skill to a huge marketplace with endless possibilities.

Customer Support

The app provides users with the opportunity to track the movement of the service provider engaged until they arrive at the location the service is to be rendered. And along with this, a team of customer support professionals are available 24/7 to assist users every step of the way.

Business Enhancement Loan
