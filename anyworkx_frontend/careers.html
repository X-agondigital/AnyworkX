About Us

AnyworkX is a digital product of X-agon Digital Solutions Limited. X-agon provides exceptional technology solutions and services that adhere to globally recognized standards and the latest technology trends. We are Afrocentric and through our offerings, we help individuals and organizations across Africa attain their business objectives thus driving productivity across the continent. We need you to make it happen!

Why you will love it here

  • We are a young organization with ambitious growth plans.
  • Working at X-agon is fun, fast paced with endless opportunities.
  • Our recruitment and reward system are based on skills and entrepreneurial skills of our people.
  • We provide you with the opportunity to create processes from scratch.
  • Opportunity to play a direct role in shaping the future of the company.
  • Opportunity to impact millions of customers and partners across African countries.
  • Opportunity to accelerate your professional growth with unique career opportunities.
  • Opportunity for mentoring, coaching and personal professional development.
  • Productivity bonus and stock option package that lets you focus on giving your best at work.
  • Flexibility of working in a hybrid mode

The DNA We Are Looking For

Passion

We want people who are passionate about what they do, committed to consistently delivering quality results and have strong belief in the vision and mission of AnyworkX.

Creativity

We value creativity and innovation and are always looking for new and better ways of doing things and solving problems.

Team Player

We believe that collaboration is key to success, so we are looking for team players who are willing to work together to achieve great goals.

Customer-focused

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do, so we are looking for individuals who are dedicated to providing exceptional customer service in all that they do.

If you think you have what it takes to join our team, we invite you to explore our open positions and submit your application. We offer opportunities for growth, personal professional development, and mentoring.

At X-agon, we want to work with bold, creative, and collaborative people who enjoy supporting those around them to succeed. It takes three steps to be a member of our team:

Step 1

Apply: X-agon is a skill-based employer. What we really look for is skill, drive, intelligence, boldness, and integrity. If you feel you are the kind of person we need for any open role, you only need to send in your resume. Our positions remain open until we find the right fit.

Step 2

Interviews and a task: If you tick some of the boxes, you will be invited for an interview either physically or virtually. After the interview you will be asked to complete a task from home. Next, you will have a final interview with a senior management team member before we make a final decision.

Step 3

Decision. If we find our corporate DNA in you and you are the right fit, the decision will always be to ask you to join our team to build a world of endless possibilities together.
