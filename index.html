Verified Personnel

Service providers and customers go through proper KYC to prevent fake identities or impersonation. Both parties determine the level of verification they are comfortable transacting business with. Service providers also go through mandatory computer-based customer service training as part of the onboarding process. The app provides customers with the opportunity to track the movement of the service provider to where the service is to be rendered.

Secured Payment Method

AnyworkX guarantees a safe and secure payment method. A wallet feature ensures seamless financial transactions between customers and service providers. payments for services rendered are upfront and escrowed in the wallet until the job is done satisfactorily.

Professionalism

AnyworkX connects you to only verified service providers who are immediately available to render the services you need whenever and wherever you need them.
